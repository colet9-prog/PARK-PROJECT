Activities:

  • Playground: Located in the northwest corner, our updated playground features separate sections for toddlers and older children (ages 2+). Open dawn to dusk.
  • Paths & Trails: Enjoy the 1-mile paved walking path circling the park or explore the natural, wooded nature trails found on the west side of the lake.
  • Picnic Area: Central picnic tables are available throughout the green space on a first-come, first-served basis. Large group reservations are recommended.
  • Sports Field: A multi-use grass field is available for informal play (soccer, frisbee, etc.). Please note that scheduled league play is not permitted.
  • Lake Observation: Benches are situated by the water for quiet observation of local wildlife. Fishing is prohibited to protect the aquatic ecosystem.
  • Restroom Facilities: Main, accessible restrooms are located next to the Sports Field building (see the Navigation Map for location).
