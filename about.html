Short description of park's history:

  • Established: July 24th, 2025.
  • Founders: Thomas Cole, Coleville City Council, and Coleville Mayor Stephenson.
  • Our Mission: To create a fun and friendly environment for every member of our community. We aim to help heal and encourage our local environment and community.
  • Milestones:
    • 2022: Park proposal submitted.
    • 2024: Park construction began in March.
    • 2024: Grand opening held on July 24th.
    • 2025: Installation of recycling and compost bins throughout the park.

Goals:

  • Preservation of nature

    We maintain a 90% native plant ratio throughout our park for our local wildlife. We also use toxin-free pest control methods to encourage a healthy ecosystem.

  • Educating the community on sustainable practices

    We have signs throughout the park that explain the importance of recycling, composting, and native plants. We also hold monthly workshops on sustainable practices for all ages.

  • Recycling, composting

    We have recycling and compost bins located throughout the park to encourage proper waste disposal.
