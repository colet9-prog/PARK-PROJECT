Welcome to Coleville's Community Park!

Established in 2024, Coleville's Community Park is your local destination for recreation and nature appreciation. Enjoy our playground, picnic areas, and beautiful paths and trails while learning about our commitment to environmental preservation and sustainable community practices. Check the highlights below for upcoming events!

Come and enjoy our playgrounds!

Come and enjoy our playgrounds! Our expansive structures for toddlers and older children are built on safe, recycled rubber mulch.

Our 2025 Turkey Trot was a great success!

Our 2025 Turkey Trot was a success! Thank you to all who participated and helped raise funds for our native plant restoration.

Enjoy a beautiful walk, jog, or run!

Enjoy a beautiful walk, jog, or run! Our three miles of paved and natural trails offer scenic views for visitors of all ages.

Mayor Stephenson celebrates success

Mayor Stephenson has come to celebrate the park's success, officially dedicating our new community composting facility.

Plenty of room for parties or playdates

Plenty of room for parties or playdates! Our dedicated picnic grove is available for reservation or spontaneous family lunches.

Relax by the lake

Find a quiet spot to relax by the lake. It's a wonderful place for bird watching, reading, or quiet contemplation.
